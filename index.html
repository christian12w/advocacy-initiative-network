About Us

The Advocacy Initiative Network is a registered organization under the Persons with Disabilities Act No. 6 of 2012, based in Lusaka, Zambia. Our headquarters are located at Compensation Worker's House, 3rd Floor, Suite 346, along Cairo Road, Lusaka.

Our motto, "Inclusion Begins with a Story," reflects our commitment to producing films that highlight the lived experiences of persons with disabilities.

Our Objectives

1 General Objective

To promote the rights, inclusion, and dignity of persons with disabilities by using film and visual storytelling as powerful tools for advocacy, awareness, and social transformation.

2 Specific Objectives

  • Produce inclusive and authentic films
  • Amplify the voices of persons with disabilities
  • Raise public awareness and challenge stereotypes
  • Promote accessibility in media and the arts
  • Influence policy and social change through storytelling
  • Foster inclusive collaborations and partnerships
  • Document and archive disability histories and cultures

Governance Structure

Advisory Board

The Advisory Board consists of 5 to 10 members, including a Chairperson, Treasurer, Secretary, and Committee Members. The board provides policy direction, approves annual work plans and budgets, and serves for a renewable two-year term.

Management/Secretariat

The organization is managed by an Executive Director, supported by a National Coordinator, Media Director, Accountant, and Administration Officer. The secretariat implements board policies and reports quarterly.
